Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how BoardroomCost ("we," "us," or "the Site") handles information when you use boardroomcost.com. The core calculator runs entirely in your browser — no salary or meeting data is ever sent to our servers.

Information we do not collect

The meeting cost calculator performs all calculations locally in your browser using JavaScript. The number of attendees, salary figures, and meeting duration you enter are not transmitted to or stored on our servers at any point.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality (such as remembering your theme preference) and for analytics purposes. Analytics data is generally aggregated and does not directly identify you.

Advertising — Google AdSense

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Contact form

If you submit a message via our contact form, the information you provide is used only to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Reach us via our contact page.
