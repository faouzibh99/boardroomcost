About us

We built the meeting cost calculator we wished existed

Most companies track software spend, travel budgets, and office rent to the dollar. The time cost of meetings — often the single largest discretionary expenditure — goes almost entirely unmeasured. BoardroomCost exists to fix that.

Why we built this

The catalyst was a simple observation: every organization we've worked with or spoken to knows that they have too many meetings and that many of them aren't worth the time they cost. Almost none of them had ever calculated the actual dollar figure — or presented it to leadership in a way that made the business case for change impossible to ignore.

We wanted a tool that did three things unusually well: calculate the true loaded cost of any meeting in real time (not just salary, but with the overhead multiplier that reflects actual employee cost), make the number viscerally clear through shock-factor framing and real-world comparisons, and give people the data they need to make the case for meeting reform to their teams and leadership.

What makes it different

Most meeting cost calculators online give you a static result after you submit a form. BoardroomCost shows you a live number that updates as you type — more like a taxi meter than a calculator. We also apply a 1.3× overhead multiplier automatically (reflecting benefits, payroll taxes, and workspace costs that most salary-only calculators ignore), and we model the ROI of meeting time reduction so you can turn the insight into a concrete proposal.

Privacy

All calculations happen in your browser. Your salary figures, team size, and meeting data are never sent to a server. We built it this way intentionally — sensitive compensation data belongs in your browser, not ours.

Private by design

All calculations run locally in your browser. No salary data is ever uploaded anywhere.

Real-time accuracy

Live cost meter with overhead multiplier — not just a salary calculation, but the true loaded cost.

Always free

No account, no paywall, no premium tier. The full calculator is free for every team, forever.
